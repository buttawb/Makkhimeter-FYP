Original Image

All acquired images were scaled at a standard size using image transformation (scaling function) to get the better results and system uniformity.

Blur & Greyscale Image

The colored image than converted into gray-scale image. Contrast enhancement processes were applied to balance the intensity level of gray-scale image.

Binary Image

The system image was converted into binary format such that all the veins are connected properly for segmentation and the unwanted pixels were removed.

Skeletonize Image

In order to connect the disconnected veins, the vein lines were become very thick. So we used, skeleton operation to capture the skull only.

Dilated & Skeletonize Image

That Skeltonized image is now dilated so that the regions are prominent.

Coloured regions with labels Image

Colours were given to each segment of the wing along with labels and then their total pixels were extracted, which were later converted to area and perimeter with the help of some math.

Coloured wing Image

The whole object was coloured to find the area and perimeter.
